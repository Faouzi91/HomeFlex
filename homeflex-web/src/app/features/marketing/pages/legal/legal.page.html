Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, phone number, payment details) and information generated by your use of the platform (booking history, search queries, messages, and device/usage data). Payment card data is never stored on HomeFlex servers — it is processed exclusively by Stripe under PCI DSS Level 1 compliance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Facilitate bookings, payments, and landlord-tenant communication.
  • Verify identity and prevent fraud (KYC via Stripe Identity).
  • Send transactional notifications (booking confirmations, payment receipts).
  • Improve search relevance and platform recommendations.
  • Comply with legal obligations under GDPR and applicable local law.

3. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your account. Booking records are kept for seven years to comply with financial regulations. You may request erasure of your data at any time from your workspace Settings page — subject to legal retention requirements.

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data (JSON export from Settings).
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data from your profile page.
  • Erasure: Delete your account and all associated data from Settings.
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format.
  • Object: Opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

5. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact us at privacy@homeflex.com.

Terms of Service

Last updated: April 23, 2026

1. Acceptance of Terms

By creating an account or using HomeFlex, you agree to these Terms of Service. If you do not agree, do not use the platform. HomeFlex reserves the right to update these terms with notice provided via email or in-app notification.

2. User Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate information during registration and KYC verification.
  • Use the platform only for lawful rental transactions.
  • Do not post fraudulent listings or submit false claims.
  • Respect other users' privacy and property.
  • Comply with all applicable local tenancy laws.

3. Payments & Fees

HomeFlex collects a 15% platform commission on each completed booking. Payments are held in escrow until the landlord approves the booking, at which point funds are released to the landlord's connected Stripe account. Refund policies depend on the landlord's cancellation policy — full refunds are issued if the landlord rejects a booking.

4. Dispute Resolution

Disputes between tenants and landlords can be filed from the Workspace → Disputes tab. HomeFlex administrators review evidence submitted by both parties and make binding decisions within 5 business days.

5. Limitation of Liability

HomeFlex acts as an intermediary marketplace. We do not own, manage, or inspect rental properties. Our liability is limited to the platform fees collected for a given transaction.

6. Account Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms, commit fraud, or otherwise harm the platform community, with or without prior notice.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. HomeFlex uses cookies to maintain authentication sessions, remember preferences, and improve platform performance.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
ACCESS_TOKEN Authentication — identifies your session 15 minutes
REFRESH_TOKEN Session renewal — keeps you signed in 7 days
XSRF-TOKEN Security — prevents cross-site request forgery Session

3. Essential Cookies Only

HomeFlex uses only essential cookies required for security and authentication. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or analytics cookies that profile your browsing behavior across other websites.

4. Managing Cookies

You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that clearing the authentication cookies will sign you out of HomeFlex. Blocking essential cookies will prevent you from using the platform.
